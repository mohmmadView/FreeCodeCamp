About Us

People and Science with Service

Sanitary Plumbing & Heating Corp. has been a leading provider of plumbing and heating services since it was founded in 1929. Even today, it’s still lead by the Clark family, brothers Harris and Jonathan Clark. They are the third generation of Clarks since their grandfather founded Sanitary 90 years ago. The company was one of the original “hyper-locals”, that is, always headquartered in New York City, today in the Bronx and all of us collectively -managers, technicians, and customers-hail from all of New York’s Boroughs. “Family” has always been one of our core values, from our team to customers. Thus our tagline: “Professionals helping Neighbors”. We take the science with service of plumbing seriously, as it, like our sister services, keeps your homes running so you can enjoy home without having to think about it.
